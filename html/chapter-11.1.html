The Compsition of Seawater

Almost anything can be found in seawater. This includes dissolved materials from
Earth's crust as well as materials released from organism. The most important
components of seawater that influence life forms are salinity, temperature,
dissolve gases (mostly oxygen and carbon dioxide), nutrients,and pH. These
elements vary in their composition as well as in their influence on marine life.
