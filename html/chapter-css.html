From Garden to Plate

A Portager is a French term for anomamental vegatable or kitchen garden. Often flowers (edible and non-edible) and herbs are planted with the vegatables to enhance the garden's beauty.The goal is to make the function of providing food aesthetically pleasing.

What to plant

Plants are chosen as much for their functionality as for their color and form.Many are trained to grow upward. A well-designed potager can provide food, cut flower and herbs for the home with very little maintance. Portager can disguise their function of providing for a home in a wide array of forms-from the carefree style of the cottage garden to the formality of a knot garden.
